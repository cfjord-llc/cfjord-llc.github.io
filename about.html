How I help

  • Align groups, organizations, and leaders on the right human-AI operating model.
  • Translate research into usable workflows, handoffs, and accountability.
  • Build training and enablement that sticks after launch.

C Fjord is built on a simple belief: AI succeeds when we design the work relationship—not just the model.

Throughout my lifelong career spanning academic and industry engagements, I’ve developed training programs, trained leaders, developed AI systems, and built novel measurement techniques to evaluate human-AI teamwork.

That mix of research and delivery experience helps me diagnose what breaks in real deployments—unclear roles, mismatched expectations, fragile handoffs, and uncalibrated trust—and then design the right interaction, collaboration, or teaming model to fix it.

What you can expect

Practical guidance that makes human-AI systems reliable, measurable, and aligned with how teams actually work.

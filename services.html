Services

C Fjord helps organizations design human-AI interaction, collaboration, and teaming—through consulting, tech design, and training. Engagements are built around role clarity, coordination, trust calibration, and measurable outcomes.

Human-AI Teaming Blueprint

A fast, practical engagement to map work-as-done, define roles and autonomy, and align the organization on how humans and AI will coordinate.

  • Workflow & risk discovery - Identify decision points, failure modes, and where teaming fits
  • Role & autonomy design - Define allocation of function, escalation, and override paths
  • Coordination protocols - Establish review rituals, accountability, and communication cues
  • Success metrics - Define measures for performance, trust calibration, and recovery from error

Interaction & Workflow Design Sprint

Design how people and AI actually work together: interfaces, handoffs, guardrails, and error recovery—built around the realities of your workflow.

  • Interaction design - Make intent legible with transparency and controllability
  • Handoff design - Define when the AI should suggest, act, or defer to humans
  • Guardrails & recovery - Design correction paths, escalation, and safe defaults
  • Artifacts - Produce workflow diagrams, specs, and prototype-ready requirements

Training & Team Enablement

Build the human capability required for teaming: shared mental models, coordination habits, and leadership alignment.

  • AI literacy for teams - Capabilities, limitations, verification, and responsible use
  • Teaming norms - Review rituals, documentation, escalation, and accountability
  • Scenario-based practice - Drills for coordination, recovery, and high-stakes edge cases
  • Manager playbooks - Practical guidance for leading adoption and calibration

Evaluation & Monitoring

Measure what matters in human-AI work: performance, trust calibration, workload, and resilience—before and after launch.

  • Metrics & instrumentation - Define what “good” looks like beyond accuracy
  • Mixed-method evaluation - Lab, field, and expert review for realistic evidence
  • Monitoring strategy - Ongoing checks for drift, adoption, and failure patterns
  • Iteration loop - Turn findings into actionable design and training updates

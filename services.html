Our Services

My services are driven by a core belief: that AI technology, when thoughtfully integrated with human potential, can revolutionize workplaces while preserving and enhancing company culture. I offer a range of consulting and training solutions designed to bridge the gap between novel technology and your existing workforce.

AI Design and Education Workshops

My education approach helps organizations understand how AI can enhance human work rather than replace it, focusing on creating a shared understanding of AI's potential:

  • Understand Human Needs First - Building the foundation for people-centered AI implementation
  • Co-Design Technology With the Workforce - Ensuring buy-in and practical relevance
  • Create Processes for Transparency - Establishing trust through clear communication
  • Iterative Improvement Focus - Teaching continuous adaptation to evolving needs

Human-First AI Workforce Training

My training framework consists of four sequential-yet-standalone programs designed to meet your organization wherever you are on your AI journey:

  • Educate Workforces on AI Technologies - Building accessible knowledge about AI capabilities relevant to specific roles
  • Identify Collaborative Workflows - Mapping processes where human strengths and AI capabilities can be optimally paired
  • Co-Create Tailored Human-AI Collaborations - Developing customized implementation strategies for specific contexts
  • Support Ongoing Adaptation - Providing continuous guidance as AI evolves in your organization

AI Opportunity Identification Audit

I help bridge humans and AI by identifying opportunities for integration that enhance your workforce capabilities through four proven interventions:

  • Conduct Organizational Needs Assessments - Mapping workflows and analyzing pain points for AI enhancement
  • Facilitate Role & Task Mapping - Distinguishing which tasks best suit humans vs. AI capabilities
  • Develop Collaboration Frameworks - Creating structured interaction models between people and systems
  • Establish Human-Centered Metrics - Measuring success by trust, satisfaction, and adaptability

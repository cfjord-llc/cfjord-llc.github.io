C Fjord is focused on one thing: helping organizations design human-AI interaction, collaboration, and teaming that holds up in real work.

Human-AI Interaction Design

Make AI understandable, controllable, and easy to correct—so people can use it confidently without slowing down.

  • 1
    Clarify Capability Boundaries

    Set expectations about what the system can do, where it fails, and when humans should verify.

  • 2
    Design for Feedback & Correction

    Make it fast to steer, edit, undo, and recover—so humans stay in control of outcomes.

  • 3
    Make Intent Legible

    Use UI cues, summaries, and provenance to explain what the AI is doing and why.

  • 4
    Plan for Failure & Recovery

    Build escalation paths and fallbacks so the workflow remains resilient under uncertainty.

Human-AI Teaming Training & Enablement

Teaming is learned. We train teams to build realistic expectations, coordinate effectively, and adapt over time as AI systems evolve.

  • 1
    Build Realistic Expectations

    Align leaders and teams on capabilities, limitations, and verification so trust stays calibrated.

  • 2
    Practice Coordination & Handoffs

    Turn workflows into training scenarios that make escalation, review, and accountability explicit.

  • 3
    Develop Team Norms

    Establish routines for prompting, reviewing, documenting decisions, and learning from errors.

  • 4
    Monitor & Adapt Over Time

    Use metrics and retrospectives to improve performance, reduce risk, and prevent capability drift.
